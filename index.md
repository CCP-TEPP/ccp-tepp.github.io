The
Project to form a
Collaborative Computational Project on Theoretical and Experimental Particle Physics
(CCP-TEPP)
is a [CoSeC][cosec]-supported project to scope the formation of a [CCP][ccp]
supporting the development and maintenance of software and software skills
in the particle physics community.

Announcements are posted to [the CCP-TEPP-ANNOUNCE list on JISCmail][mailinglist].
Please sign up there to stay in the loop.

Please visit our [announcements][announcements] and [events][events] pages
for more information.
