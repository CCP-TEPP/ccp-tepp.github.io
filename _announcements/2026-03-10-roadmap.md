The [_Roadmap for Software for UK Particle Physics_][documents]
was drafted by CCP-TEPP in 2025&ndash;2026,
based on a series of workshops
held with the particle physics software community through 2025.

This document is now available from the [Documents section][documents] of this site.

There will be additional workshops later in 2026
to review and update the document based on CCP-TEPP's progress this year.
